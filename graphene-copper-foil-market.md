Monolayer graphene consists of a single layer of graphene, bilayer graphene has two layers, and multilayer graphene includes more than two layers. Each type offers different properties and applications, with monolayer graphene being the thinnest and most flexible, bilayer graphene showing unique electronic properties, and multilayer graphene exhibiting enhanced mechanical strength and conductivity.</p></p>
