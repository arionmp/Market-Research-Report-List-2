<p><p>Building Stone Veneer market is divided into two main types, namely Faux Stone Veneer and Natural Stone Veneer. Faux Stone Veneer is a manufactured product that mimics the look of natural stone but is lighter and more affordable. It is often made from materials like concrete, polyurethane, or foam. On the other hand, Natural Stone Veneer is made from real stone materials such as granite, slate, or limestone. It provides a more authentic and high-end appearance, but comes with a higher price tag compared to faux stone veneer.</p></p>
