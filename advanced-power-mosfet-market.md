<p><ul><li>SiC</li><li>GaN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Power MOSFET market includes two types of advanced materials: Silicon Carbide (SiC) and Gallium Nitride (GaN). SiC MOSFETs offer high breakdown voltage and low on-resistance, making them ideal for high-power applications. On the other hand, GaN MOSFETs provide high switching frequency and efficiency, suitable for high-speed applications. Both SiC and GaN technologies are gaining popularity in various industries such as automotive, power electronics, and telecommunications due to their superior performance characteristics compared to traditional silicon MOSFETs.</p></p>
