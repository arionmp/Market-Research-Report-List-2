<p><p>Depth filtration media market types include activated carbon, diatomaceous earth, cellulose, and perlite. Activated carbon is known for its ability to adsorb impurities, making it popular in water and air filtration systems. Diatomaceous earth is a natural material composed of fossilized algae, offering high filtration efficiency. Cellulose media is derived from wood pulp and is cost-effective for general filtration applications. Perlite is a volcanic rock that is lightweight and has high porosity, making it suitable for filtration of liquids and gases.</p></p>
