<p><p>Syringe infusion pumps are medical devices used to deliver precise amounts of fluids or medications into a patient's body. The market for these pumps is divided into two main types: single-channel and dual-channel pumps. Single-channel pumps have only one syringe barrel and are typically used for infusing one medication at a time, while dual-channel pumps have two separate syringe barrels and can simultaneously deliver two different medications. Both types of pumps are essential in healthcare settings for accurate and controlled drug delivery.</p></p>
