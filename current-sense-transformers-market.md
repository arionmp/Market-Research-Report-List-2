<p><p>Current sense transformers are used for various applications such as mutual inductance measuring current and protective current transformers. Mutual inductance measuring current transformers are designed to accurately measure current flow in electrical systems by using the principle of mutual inductance. Protective current transformers, on the other hand, are used to detect faults and protect equipment by monitoring current levels and providing feedback to control systems. Both types of transformers play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical systems.</p></p>
