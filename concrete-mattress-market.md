<p><p>Concrete mattresses are commonly used in erosion control and pipeline protection projects. There are three main types in the market: rigid, flexible, and hybrid. Rigid concrete mattresses provide excellent stability and protection in high-flow areas. Flexible mattresses are ideal for uneven or irregular surfaces, offering flexibility and durability. Hybrid mattresses combine the benefits of both rigid and flexible types, providing a versatile solution for various project requirements. Each type caters to specific needs in the construction and protection of underwater infrastructure.</p></p>
