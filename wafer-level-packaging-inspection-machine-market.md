<p><p>Wafer level packaging inspection machines can be categorized based on technology type, such as E-beam inspection or optical inspection. E-beam inspection uses an electron beam to detect defects at a very small scale, while optical inspection relies on light to examine the surface of wafers. These machines can also be classified based on system type, including patterned systems which analyze structured patterns, and non-patterned systems which inspect random defects on wafers.</p></p>
