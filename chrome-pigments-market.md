<p><p>Chrome Pigments are commonly classified into two categories - Organic and Inorganic pigments. Organic pigments are derived from carbon-based compounds and are known for their bright and vibrant colors. They are often used in applications like printing inks, plastics, and cosmetics. Inorganic pigments, on the other hand, are mineral-based and are valued for their durability and resistance to heat and light. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, automotive, and ceramics. Both types of pigments play a vital role in various industries around the world.</p></p>
