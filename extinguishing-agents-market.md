<p><p>Extinguishing agents market comprises various types such as Halotron, foam, carbon dioxide, dry chemical, wet chemical, and others. Halotron extinguishing agents are popular for being clean, non-conductive, and eco-friendly. Foam extinguishing agents are effective for smothering fires and preventing re-ignition. Carbon dioxide extinguishing agents starve a fire of oxygen. Dry chemical agents are useful for combating flammable liquid and electrical fires. Wet chemical agents are ideal for cooking oil fires. Other types include water mist, clean agent, and emulsifiers. Each type offers unique properties for different fire scenarios.</p></p>
