<p><p>Polyurethane synthetic leather market types can be divided into dry synthetic and wet synthetic. Dry synthetic leather is created by layering a PU coating onto a non-woven fabric, giving it a dry and matte finish. On the other hand, wet synthetic leather is produced by applying a PU coating onto a woven fabric, resulting in a glossy and shiny finish. Both types offer a durable and cost-effective alternative to real leather, catering to different preferences in terms of appearance and texture.</p></p>
