<p><p>ICP (Inductively Coupled Plasma) Nebulizer is a device used in Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) to introduce the sample into the plasma for analysis. The nebulizer converts the liquid sample into a fine aerosol which is then mixed with argon gas and injected into the plasma torch for ionization. ICP Nebulizers play a crucial role in ICP-MS analysis by ensuring precise and accurate sample introduction.</p><p>The global ICP Nebulizer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing adoption of ICP-MS in various industries such as environmental testing, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and mining.
