<p><p>Bilirubin assay kits are used to measure the levels of bilirubin in the blood. There are different types of assays available in the market, including the Diazonium method and the Vanadate oxidation method. The Diazonium method involves the reaction of bilirubin with a reagent containing diazonium salts, while the Vanadate oxidation method uses vanadate as an oxidizing agent. These kits are essential for diagnosing liver and bile disorders, as abnormal bilirubin levels can indicate liver damage or other health issues.</p></p>
