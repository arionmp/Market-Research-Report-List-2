<p><p>Masonry veneer market consists of different types such as adhered veneer, anchored veneer, articulated veneer, and others. Adhered veneer is attached to the substrate using adhesives, while anchored veneer is secured with metal ties or anchors. Articulated veneer utilizes a combination of mechanical connections and adhesives for installation. Other types may include prefabricated panels or systems that offer unique installation methods. Each type offers distinct benefits and aesthetics, catering to various architectural needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
