<p><p>Zoom cinema lenses allow filmmakers to easily change the focal length of the lens while shooting, providing flexibility in framing shots. Two main types of zoom cinema lenses are prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, which allows for high image quality and wider apertures, ideal for capturing crisp, detailed shots. Zoom lenses, on the other hand, offer a range of focal lengths in one lens, making them versatile for capturing a variety of shots without having to switch lenses.</p></p>
