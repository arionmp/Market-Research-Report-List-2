<p><p>Complete rotary interfaces are devices that allow for the transfer of data or power between rotating and stationary parts. Single channel rotary interfaces have one channel for transferring signals or power. Double channel rotary interfaces have two separate channels for transferring data or power. Multi-channel rotary interfaces have multiple channels for transferring data or power simultaneously. Each of these market types caters to different needs depending on the complexity and number of signals that need to be transferred between rotating and stationary components.</p></p>
