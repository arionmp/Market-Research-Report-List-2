<p><p>Work-Class ROVs are categorized into two main types: Light Class and Heavy Class. Light Class ROVs are compact, agile and typically used for inspections, maintenance, and light intervention tasks in shallower waters. They are ideal for tasks that require a smaller and more maneuverable vehicle. On the other hand, Heavy Class ROVs are larger, more powerful, and designed for more demanding operations in deeper waters. They are equipped with advanced technology and heavy-duty capabilities for tasks such as construction, drilling support, and deep-sea exploration.</p></p>
