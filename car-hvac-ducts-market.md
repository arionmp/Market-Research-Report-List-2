<p><p>Car HVAC ducts come in two main types: gas ducts and liquid ducts. Gas ducts are used to distribute air throughout the vehicle's heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system. They help regulate the temperature inside the car by circulating hot or cold air as needed. On the other hand, liquid ducts are designed to transport coolant or refrigerant throughout the system to ensure efficient cooling or heating. Both types of ducts play a crucial role in maintaining a comfortable interior climate in vehicles.</p></p>
