<p><ul><li>Single Embossed</li><li>Double Embossed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pillow Plate Heat Exchangers come in two main types: Single Embossed and Double Embossed. Single Embossed plates have a single embossment on one side of the plate, allowing for efficient heat transfer between the two fluids. Double Embossed plates have embossments on both sides, providing even higher heat transfer rates. Both types offer advantages in different applications, such as refrigeration, food processing, and pharmaceutical industries. The choice between Single and Double Embossed plates depends on the specific requirements of the heat exchanger system.</p></p>
