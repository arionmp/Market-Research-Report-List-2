<p><p>Road graders are heavy equipment machines used for leveling, spreading, and crowning roads. There are two main types in the market: Rigid Frame Road Graders and Articulated Frame Road Graders. Rigid Frame Road Graders have a single frame that does not pivot, providing stability but limited maneuverability. Articulated Frame Road Graders have a pivot point between the front and rear frames, allowing for greater flexibility in tight spaces. Customers can choose between these two types based on their specific needs and project requirements.</p></p>
