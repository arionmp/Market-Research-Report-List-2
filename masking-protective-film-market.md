<p><p>Masking Protective Film is a type of material used to protect surfaces during various processes like painting, polishing, or transportation. There are two main types of Masking Protective Film: Solid and Liquid. Solid Masking Protective Film is a pre-cut adhesive film that can be easily applied to surfaces and removed without leaving any residue. Liquid Masking Protective Film, on the other hand, is a liquid spray that forms a protective barrier when applied and can be easily peeled off once the work is complete. Both types provide effective protection for surfaces against damage.</p></p>
