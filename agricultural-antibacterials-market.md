<p><p>The Agricultural Antibacterials market includes various types of formulations such as liquid, liquid-dispersible granules, and wettable powder. Liquid formulations are easy to apply and quickly absorbed by plants, providing rapid antibacterial action. Liquid-dispersible granules are soluble in water, making them convenient for foliar applications. Wettable powders are finely ground particles that disperse in water, offering broad coverage and sustained antibacterial protection. Each formulation type has its own advantages in terms of ease of application, effectiveness, and persistence in the field.</p></p>
