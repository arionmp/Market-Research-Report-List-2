On the other hand, Two Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps are designed for applications that require higher vacuum levels. They are equipped with two sets of vanes and two separate chambers for improved efficiency and higher vacuum levels. Both types cater to different vacuum requirements in various industries.</p></p>
