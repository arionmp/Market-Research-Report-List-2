<p><p>Industrial gantry robots are classified into two main types based on their structure: Open Gantry Robot and Closed Gantry Robot. Open gantry robots feature a more simple and accessible design, making them suitable for applications that require flexibility and quick changes. Closed gantry robots, on the other hand, have a more enclosed structure, offering higher precision and reliability for applications that demand utmost accuracy and consistency. Both types cater to different industrial needs, providing a wide range of options for automation solutions.</p></p>
