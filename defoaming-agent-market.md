<p><p>Defoaming agents are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their application areas. Type I defoaming agents are primarily used in industrial applications such as paints, coatings, and oil & gas industries. They are designed to quickly eliminate foam and prevent re-formation. On the other hand, Type II defoaming agents are more suitable for consumer products like detergents, food & beverages, and personal care products. They are formulated to be effective at lower concentrations and exhibit long-lasting performance in reducing foam.</p></p>
