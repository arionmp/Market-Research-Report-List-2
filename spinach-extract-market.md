<p><p>Spinach extract market includes two main types - powder extract and solid extract. Powder extract is made by drying and grinding spinach leaves into a fine powder, which can be easily added to various food and beverage products for its nutritional benefits. On the other hand, solid extract is produced by concentrating the active components of spinach through a process of extraction and evaporation, resulting in a more potent form that can be used in dietary supplements and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to different market segments looking for convenient ways to incorporate the health benefits of spinach into their diet.</p></p>
