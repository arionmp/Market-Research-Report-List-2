<p><strong>The Green Roof Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layered Green Roof Systems</li><li>Modular Green Roof Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Green Roof Systems have two main types, Layered Green Roof Systems and Modular Green Roof Systems. Layered Green Roof Systems involve a series of layers including a waterproof membrane, insulation, drainage system, growing medium, and vegetation. On the other hand, Modular Green Roof Systems are pre-planted modules that can be easily installed and removed, allowing for more flexibility and streamlined maintenance. Both systems provide numerous environmental benefits such as reducing stormwater runoff, improving air quality, and enhancing insulation properties.</p></p>
