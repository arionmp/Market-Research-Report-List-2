The two main types of IOLs are monofocal and multifocal. Monofocal IOLs provide clear vision at one fixed distance, usually for distance vision, while multifocal IOLs allow for clear vision at multiple distances, reducing the need for glasses. Both types of IOLs are commonly used in cataract surgery to improve vision.</p></p>
