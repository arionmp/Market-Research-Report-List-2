<p><p>Meat starter cultures play a crucial role in the fermentation process of meat products. Maturation starters are used to enhance flavor, texture, and overall quality of fermented meats by promoting the breakdown of proteins and lipids. Surface starters are responsible for creating the characteristic aroma and taste of fermented meats by producing specific enzymes and organic acids during the fermentation process. Both types of starter cultures are essential in ensuring the desired characteristics and quality of fermented meat products.</p></p>
