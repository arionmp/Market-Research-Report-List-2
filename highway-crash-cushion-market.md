<p><p>Highway Crash Cushions come in two main types: Non-redirective and Redirective Crash Cushions. Non-redirective Crash Cushions are designed to absorb the impact of a crash without redirecting the vehicle, thus minimizing damage to the vehicle and reducing the risk of injury to occupants. Redirective Crash Cushions, on the other hand, are designed to redirect the vehicle away from obstacles or hazards, further reducing the risk of injury and damage. Both types play a crucial role in improving highway safety and reducing the severity of crashes.</p></p>
