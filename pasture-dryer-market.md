<p><p>Pasture dryers are available in two main market types: stationary and mobile. Stationary pasture dryers are fixed in one location, typically on a farm or ranch, and are ideal for drying large amounts of pasture in a single area. Mobile pasture dryers, on the other hand, are portable and can be easily transported to different locations, making them suitable for smaller farms or those needing to dry pasture in various areas. Both types of pasture dryers serve the purpose of efficiently drying grass and forage to preserve nutrients for livestock feed.</p></p>
