<p><p>COVID-19 Antibody Detection Kits come in various types such as Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting antibodies in blood samples. ELISA kits use an enzyme to detect antibodies and are commonly used in laboratories. Neutralization assays measure the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type of kit plays a crucial role in identifying and monitoring the spread of COVID-19 in different settings.</p></p>
