<p><strong>The Liquid Applied Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Applied Membranes are primarily categorized into four types: Bituminous, Elastomeric, Cementitious, and Other. Bituminous membranes are made from bitumen and provide excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric membranes are flexible and can stretch to accommodate movement in the substrate. Cementitious membranes are made from cement and provide high chemical resistance. The Other category includes membranes made from various other materials like polyurethane or epoxy. Each type of membrane has its own specific applications and advantages in the construction and waterproofing industry.</p></p>
