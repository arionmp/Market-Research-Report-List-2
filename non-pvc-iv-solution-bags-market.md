<p><p>Non PVC IV solution bags are available in two main types: single-chambered and multi-chambered. Single-chambered bags are designed for storing and delivering one type of medication or solution at a time, making them ideal for simpler infusion needs. On the other hand, multi-chambered bags have multiple compartments that can hold different medications separately, allowing for compatibility of multiple drugs in one infusion. This feature is particularly useful for patients requiring simultaneous administration of different medications or solutions.</p></p>
