<p><p>Milk fat fractions are categorized into different types based on their melting points. Low melting fractions have a melting point below body temperature and are used in products like ice cream and spreads. Medium melting fractions have a melting point closer to body temperature and are used in chocolates and bakery items. High melting fractions have a melting point above body temperature and are used in products like coatings and fillings. Each type of fraction serves a specific purpose in various food applications.</p></p>
