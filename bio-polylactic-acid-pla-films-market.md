Sol-Gel involves a solution-based process for coating thin films, ALD utilizes a layer-by-layer deposition technique to achieve precise control over film thickness, and Multilayer involves the deposition of multiple layers of different materials to enhance film properties. The PLA film market offers a sustainable and recyclable alternative to traditional plastic packaging materials.</p></p>
