<p><strong>The Solar Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Solar Inverter</li><li>String Solar Inverter</li><li>Micro Solar Inverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central Solar Inverters are the most common type, used for large-scale solar power plants. They convert DC energy from multiple PV arrays into AC electricity for grid connection. String Solar Inverters are used for medium-sized solar installations, converting DC power from a string of solar panels into usable AC power. Micro Solar Inverters are used for small residential systems, converting DC energy from individual solar panels into AC electricity. Each type serves a specific market segment based on installation size and power output requirements.</p></p>
