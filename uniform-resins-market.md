<p><p>Uniform resins are essential components in water treatment processes. The cation resin market focuses on resins that primarily remove positively charged ions from water, while the anion resin market deals with resins that target negatively charged ions. Both types of resins play a crucial role in achieving water purification and softening. Cation resins exchange ions like calcium and magnesium with sodium, while anion resins remove impurities like nitrates and sulfates. These markets cater to industries and households in need of reliable water treatment solutions.</p></p>
