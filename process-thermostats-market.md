<p><ul><li>Line-voltage Thermostat</li><li>Low-voltage Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Process thermostats are classified into two main types: line-voltage thermostats and low-voltage thermostats. Line-voltage thermostats are directly connected to the power supply and are commonly used in systems like electric baseboard heaters. On the other hand, low-voltage thermostats require a separate transformer to operate and are commonly found in HVAC systems. Both types of thermostats play a crucial role in regulating temperature in various industrial and residential settings, providing efficient and precise control over heating and cooling systems.</p></p>
