<p><p>Snowboard jackets come in three main types - insulated, shell, and 3-in-1. Insulated jackets are lined with warmth-trapping material, making them ideal for cold weather. Shell jackets are lightweight and provide protection from wind and moisture, making them suitable for mild conditions or layering. 3-in-1 jackets combine an outer shell and a removable inner liner, offering versatility for varying weather conditions.
