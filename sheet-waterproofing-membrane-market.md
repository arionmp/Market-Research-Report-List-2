<p><p>Sheet waterproofing membranes are commonly categorized into two types: Polymer Modified Bitumen Membranes and Synthetic Polymer Sheet Waterproofing Membranes. Polymer Modified Bitumen Membranes are made by blending bitumen with polymers to enhance their performance and durability. On the other hand, Synthetic Polymer Sheet Waterproofing Membranes are manufactured using various types of synthetic polymers such as PVC, TPO, and EPDM to provide excellent waterproofing properties. Both types of membranes are widely used in construction projects to protect buildings from water damage.</p></p>
