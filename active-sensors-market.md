<p><p>Active sensors are devices that emit energy to be used in data collection. Laser fluorescence sensors use a laser to excite molecules, causing them to emit fluorescent light which is then detected to analyze different substances. Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) uses radio waves to create high-resolution images of the Earth's surface by capturing radar data as the sensor moves along a path. Other types of active sensors include lidar, sonar, and radar altimeters, which are used for various applications such as remote sensing, navigation, and monitoring.</p></p>
