<p><p>The automotive exhaust catalytic materials market consists of three main types: common metal, metal oxide, and precious metals. Common metal catalysts are typically made of base metals like nickel, copper, and iron. Metal oxide catalysts utilize oxides of metals such as titanium and cerium. Precious metals, such as platinum, palladium, and rhodium, are highly effective in catalyzing emissions. Each type of catalyst has its own strengths and applications, with precious metals being the most efficient but also the most expensive option.</p></p>
