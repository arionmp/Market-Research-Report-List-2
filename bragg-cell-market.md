<p><p>Bragg Cell technology is widely used in various applications such as Q-switching, Regenerative amplifiers, Modelocking, Cavity dumping, and Laser Doppler vibrometers. In Q-switching, Bragg Cells are used to rapidly switch the laser output on and off, yielding high-power pulses. Regenerative amplifiers employ Bragg Cells to amplify the laser pulse in a feedback loop. Modelocking uses Bragg Cells to generate ultra-short pulses. Cavity dumping involves releasing the stored energy from the laser cavity using Bragg Cells. Laser Doppler vibrometers use Bragg Cells to measure vibrations with high precision.</p></p>
