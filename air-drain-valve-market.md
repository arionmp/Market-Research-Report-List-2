<p><p>Air drain valves come in three main types: Fully Automatic, Semi-Automatic, and Manual. Fully Automatic valves use electronic sensors to detect air and release it automatically, making them low maintenance and efficient. Semi-Automatic valves require some manual intervention to release air, but still offer convenient operation. Manual valves require regular monitoring and manual adjustment to release trapped air. Each type of valve caters to different needs and preferences, offering a range of options for air drainage in various systems.</p></p>
