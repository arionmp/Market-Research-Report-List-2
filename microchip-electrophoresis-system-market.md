<p><p>Microchip electrophoresis systems are classified into three main types based on the level of automation: Fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic systems are designed to perform all steps of the electrophoresis process without human intervention, providing convenience and efficiency. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention, such as sample loading or data analysis, while still automating the majority of the process. Manual systems rely on the user to perform all steps of the electrophoresis process, offering more control but requiring more time and effort.</p></p>
