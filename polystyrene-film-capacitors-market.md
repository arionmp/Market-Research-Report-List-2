<p><p>Polystyrene film capacitors are classified into two main types: Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors and Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors. Axial Polystyrene Film Capacitors are designed with leads that extend from each end of the capacitor, allowing for easy insertion into circuit boards. On the other hand, Radial Polystyrene Film Capacitors have leads that are attached to one side of the capacitor, enabling them to be easily mounted onto circuit boards. These two types cater to different preferences and requirements in the market, offering versatility in applications.</p></p>
