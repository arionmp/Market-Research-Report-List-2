<p><ul><li>Fixed-installation projectors</li><li>Pico projectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed-installation projectors are typically larger, high-brightness projectors used in permanent installations such as classrooms, auditoriums, and conference rooms. These projectors offer high image quality and advanced features such as lens shift and keystone correction. </p><p>Pico projectors, on the other hand, are small, portable projectors designed for on-the-go use. They are lightweight and compact, making them ideal for use in small meeting rooms, home entertainment, and outdoor events. Despite their smaller size, Pico projectors can still deliver impressive image quality and brightness.</p></p>
