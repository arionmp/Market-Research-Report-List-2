<p><p>In the Infectious Disease Testing Device Market, there are three main types of testing devices - Molecular Diagnostic Test, POCT (Point-of-Care Testing), and Immunodiagnostic Test market. Molecular Diagnostic Tests involve examining nucleic acids for pathogens. POCT devices provide rapid results at the patient's location, allowing immediate treatment. Immunodiagnostic Tests detect antibodies or antigens in the body's immune response to infections. Each type of testing device serves a specific purpose in diagnosing infectious diseases quickly and accurately.</p></p>
