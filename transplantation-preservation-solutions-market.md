<p><p>Transplantation Preservation Solutions market includes various types such as Static Cold Storage, Hypothermic Perfusion Preservation, and Others. Static Cold Storage involves storing organs at low temperatures without any perfusion. Hypothermic Perfusion Preservation involves continuously perfusing organs with a cold preservation solution to maintain their viability. Other preservation methods may include normothermic machine perfusion or oxygen persufflation. Each method has its own advantages and limitations in the preservation and transportation of organs for transplantation.</p></p>
