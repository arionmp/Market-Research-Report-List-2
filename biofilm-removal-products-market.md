<p><p>Biofilm removal products are categorized into three main types based on their pH levels: Neutral biofilm removal products, Alkaline biofilm removal products, and Acid biofilm removal products. Neutral biofilm removal products have a balanced pH level, making them suitable for a wide range of surfaces. Alkaline biofilm removal products are effective for breaking down organic matter and protein-based biofilms. Acid biofilm removal products are suitable for removing mineral-based biofilms and are particularly useful for descaling and removing hard water deposits.</p></p>
