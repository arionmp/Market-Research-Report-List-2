<p><p>Vehicle Radar Test Systems (VRTS) market includes various types of equipment such as Vector Signal Transceiver (VST), Variable Delay Generator (VDG), PXI Controller, and Antennae. VSTs are used for generating and analyzing radar signals, VDGs are used for introducing delay in signal paths for testing, PXI Controllers are used for controlling the overall test system, and Antennae are used for transmitting and receiving radar signals. These components collectively provide a comprehensive testing solution for vehicle radar systems ensuring their accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
