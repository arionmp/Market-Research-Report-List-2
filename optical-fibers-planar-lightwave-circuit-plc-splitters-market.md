<p><p>Optical fibers Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) Splitters are used to divide an optical signal into multiple outputs. There are two main types of PLC Splitters: 1×N PLC Splitters, where the signal is split into N outputs, and 2×N PLC Splitters, where the signal is split into two sets of N outputs. 1×N Splitters are commonly used in simple point-to-multipoint configurations, while 2×N Splitters are utilized in more complex networks requiring multiple stages of splitting. Each type offers different benefits and applications depending on the specific network requirements.</p></p>
