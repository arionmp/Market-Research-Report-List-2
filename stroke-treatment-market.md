<p><strong>The Stroke Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ischemic Stroke</li><li>Hemorrhagic Stroke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stroke treatment market consists of two main types: Ischemic Stroke and Hemorrhagic Stroke. Ischemic Stroke occurs when a blood clot blocks an artery in the brain, cutting off blood flow and causing damage to brain tissue. Treatment for Ischemic Stroke typically involves medication to dissolve the clot or surgery to remove it. Hemorrhagic Stroke, on the other hand, occurs when a blood vessel in the brain bursts, causing bleeding and damage to surrounding tissue. Treatment for Hemorrhagic Stroke often involves surgery to repair the damaged blood vessel.</p></p>
