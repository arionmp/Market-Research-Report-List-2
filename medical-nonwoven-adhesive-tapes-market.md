<p><p>Medical nonwoven adhesive tapes have two main types - double-sided and single-sided. Double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to adhere to two surfaces at once. They are commonly used for medical devices and wound dressings. Single-sided tapes have adhesive on only one side, making them suitable for applications where only one surface needs to be secured. Both types of tapes are important in the healthcare industry for their secure and reliable adhesive properties.</p></p>
