<p><ul><li>Spring Type</li><li>Direct Lever Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring type lever espresso machines operate by using a spring mechanism to maintain consistent pressure during the brewing process. This type of machine is typically easier to operate and requires less manual effort. On the other hand, direct lever espresso machines require the user to manually control the pressure by pulling down on a lever. This type of machine offers more control over the brewing process but requires more skill and practice to achieve consistent results. Both types cater to different preferences in terms of ease of use and control.</p></p>
