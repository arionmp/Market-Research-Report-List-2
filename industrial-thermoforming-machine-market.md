<p><p>Industrial thermoforming machines come in three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic machines require little to no human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. Semi-automatic machines require some human involvement, such as loading materials or removing finished products. Manual machines require complete human operation and are typically used for small-scale production or prototyping. Each type of machine offers different levels of efficiency, control, and production capacity, allowing manufacturers to choose the best option based on their specific needs and budget.</p></p>
