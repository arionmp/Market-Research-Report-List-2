<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPM) are integrated modules that combine power semiconductors, gate drivers, and protection features in a single package. The two main types of IPMs are IGBT-IPM and MOSFET-IPM. IGBT-IPMs use insulated gate bipolar transistors for higher power applications, while MOSFET-IPMs utilize metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors for lower power applications. Both types of IPMs offer advantages such as improved efficiency, higher reliability, and compact design, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial and consumer electronic applications.</p></p>
