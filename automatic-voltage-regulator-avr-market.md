<p><p>The Automatic Voltage Regulator (AVR) market consists of various types, including linear regulators, shunt voltage regulators, and others. Linear regulators provide a constant output voltage by dissipating excess power as heat. Shunt voltage regulators maintain a constant output voltage by shunting excess current to ground. Other types of AVR may include switching regulators or programmable regulators, which offer different ways to regulate voltage levels efficiently. Each type of AVR caters to specific applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
