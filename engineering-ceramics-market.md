<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Engineering Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidic Engineering Ceramics</li><li>Mixed Engineering Ceramics</li><li>Non-Oxidic Engineering Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineering ceramics can be categorized into three main types based on their composition:</p><p>1. Oxidic Engineering Ceramics: These ceramics are composed of oxides such as alumina, zirconia, and silica. They have high mechanical strength, excellent corrosion resistance, and thermal stability.</p><p>2. Mixed Engineering Ceramics: These ceramics are a combination of oxides and non-oxides such as carbides, nitrides, and borides. They exhibit superior wear resistance, high hardness, and good thermal conductivity.</p><p>3. Non-Oxidic Engineering Ceramics: These ceramics are composed of non-oxides like carbides, nitrides, and borides. They offer high chemical stability, excellent hardness, and low thermal conductivity.</p></p>
