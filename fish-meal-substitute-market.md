Additionally, technological advancements in alternative protein production and increasing investments in research and development activities are further driving the growth of the fish meal substitute market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the fish meal substitute market include the development of novel protein sources for aquafeed, the introduction of customized fish meal substitute blends for different species of fish, and the focus on improving the nutritional profile and digestibility of fish meal substitutes.
