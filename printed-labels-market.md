<p><p>Printed labels market includes various types such as wet glued labels, pressure sensitive labels, shrink sleeves, stretch sleeves, pre-gummed labels, in-mould labels, and others. Wet glued labels are applied using glue, pressure sensitive labels have adhesive backing for easy application, shrink sleeves shrink to fit the product's container, stretch sleeves are applied by stretching over the container, pre-gummed labels have adhesive already applied, in-mould labels are integrated into the product during manufacturing. Other types include wraparound labels, booklet labels, and heat transfer labels.</p></p>
