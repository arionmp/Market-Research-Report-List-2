<p><p>Jewel bearings are commonly classified into four main types: sapphire, ruby, zirconia, and others. Sapphire bearings are known for their hardness and durability, making them ideal for high precision applications. Ruby bearings are also highly resistant to wear and corrosion, commonly used in watchmaking and aerospace industries. Zirconia bearings offer excellent chemical and heat resistance. Other types of jewel bearings may include materials like ceramic or glass, each offering unique properties for specific applications in industries such as medical devices and instrumentation.</p></p>
