<p><p>Microinverters are devices used in solar power systems to convert direct current (DC) generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) for use in homes and businesses. The market for microinverters can be divided into two main types: standalone and on-grid. Standalone microinverters are used in off-grid solar systems, providing power to locations that are not connected to the utility grid. On-grid microinverters are connected to a utility grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back into the grid and potentially earn credits or compensation for the owner.</p></p>
