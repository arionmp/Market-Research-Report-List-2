<p><strong>The Switchable PDLC Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Adhesive Film</li><li>None-Adhesive Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switchable PDLC Film is available in two main market types: Self-Adhesive Film and Non-Adhesive Film. Self-Adhesive Film is designed to adhere directly to windows or glass surfaces without the need for additional adhesives, making it a convenient and easy-to-install option for both residential and commercial applications. Non-Adhesive Film, on the other hand, requires a separate adhesive layer to be applied to the film before installation. Both types offer the same switchable privacy and light control benefits, but differ in their installation methods.</p></p>
