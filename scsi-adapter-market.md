<p><ul><li>Client Side</li><li>Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the SCSI adapter market, there are two main types: client-side and server-side adapters. Client-side adapters are used in individual computers to connect peripheral devices like printers and scanners. Server-side adapters, on the other hand, are used in servers to manage large amounts of data flow between multiple devices. Client-side adapters are more common in personal computers and smaller networks, while server-side adapters are essential for data centers and enterprise servers to ensure high performance and efficient data management.</p></p>
