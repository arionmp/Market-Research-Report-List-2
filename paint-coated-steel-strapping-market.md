<p><strong>The Paint-Coated Steel Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oscillated Wound Coil</li><li>Ribbon Would Coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint-coated steel strapping is available in two main market types: oscillated wound coil and ribbon wound coil. Oscillated wound coil steel strapping is wound in a tight, compact spiral, allowing for more footage in each coil and making it easier to handle. Ribbon wound coil steel strapping is wound in a flat ribbon-like pattern, which reduces strap tangling and allows for easy dispensing. Both market types have their own unique features and benefits, catering to different packaging and strapping requirements.</p></p>
