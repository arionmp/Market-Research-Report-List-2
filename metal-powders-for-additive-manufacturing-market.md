<p><strong>The Metal Powders for Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed</li><li>Blown Powder</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal powders for additive manufacturing market can be classified into three types: Powder Bed, Blown Powder, and Others. Powder Bed involves spreading a thin layer of metal powder onto a build platform and fusing it with a heat source to create layers of the desired shape. Blown Powder, on the other hand, uses a nozzle to spray metal powder onto a surface and then melts it with a laser or electron beam. The Others category encompasses various other methods and techniques for using metal powders in additive manufacturing processes.</p></p>
