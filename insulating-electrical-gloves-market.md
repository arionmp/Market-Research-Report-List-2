Medium Voltage gloves can handle voltages ranging from 1000V to 17,000V, suitable for intermediate electrical tasks. High Voltage gloves are capable of withstanding voltages above 17,000V, used for high-risk electrical work. Each type of glove provides specific protection levels for different voltage requirements in the electrical industry.</p></p>
