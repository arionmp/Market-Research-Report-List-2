<p><strong>The Automotive Auxiliary Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Based Auxiliary Heaters</li><li>Electrical / PTC Auxiliary Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel-based auxiliary heaters use diesel or gasoline as fuel to generate heat in the vehicle. They are commonly used in regions with extreme cold temperatures and provide quick and efficient warming of the vehicle's interior.</p><p>Electrical/PTC auxiliary heaters, on the other hand, use electricity to generate heat through a Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) heating element. They are energy-efficient and provide consistent heating without the need for fuel. These heaters are gaining popularity due to their environmentally friendly nature and ease of use.</p></p>
