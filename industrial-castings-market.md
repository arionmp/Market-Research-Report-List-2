<p><p>Industrial castings market can be classified into two main types: ferrous and non-ferrous. Ferrous castings are made from iron and its alloys, such as steel, while non-ferrous castings are made from metals like aluminum, copper, zinc, and brass. Ferrous castings are known for their strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications such as automotive and construction. Non-ferrous castings, on the other hand, are valued for their corrosion resistance, conductivity, and lightweight properties, making them suitable for industries like aerospace and electronics.</p></p>
