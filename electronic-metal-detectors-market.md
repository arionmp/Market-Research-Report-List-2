<p><strong>The Electronic Metal Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low Frequency</li><li>Pulse Induction</li><li>Beat-frequency Oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Metal Detectors are used for various purposes such as security screening, archaeological exploration, and industrial metal detection. The three main types of metal detectors in the market are Very Low Frequency (VLF), Pulse Induction, and Beat-frequency Oscillation. VLF detectors are the most common type and are suitable for general purpose metal detection. Pulse Induction detectors are known for their depth capability and are often used in underwater exploration. Beat-frequency Oscillation detectors are typically simpler in design and are known for their sensitivity to smaller metal objects.</p></p>
