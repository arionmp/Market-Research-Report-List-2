<p><p>Halogen-free solder paste refers to solder paste that does not contain halogen elements like chlorine and bromine, which can release harmful gases when exposed to high temperatures during soldering. There are two main types of halogen-free solder paste: lead-free and leaded solder paste. Lead-free solder paste is made without lead, while leaded solder paste contains lead. Both types are used in electronics manufacturing, with lead-free solder paste being more environmentally friendly but sometimes requiring higher temperatures for soldering compared to leaded solder paste.</p></p>
