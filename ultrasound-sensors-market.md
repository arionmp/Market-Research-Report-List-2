<p><strong>The Ultrasound Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Sensors</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Retro-Reflective Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound sensors are commonly classified into three main types: beam sensors, proximity sensors, and ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors. Beam sensors are used for detecting the presence or absence of an object within a specific range. Proximity sensors measure the distance between the sensor and an object, providing accurate proximity information. Ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors operate by emitting sound waves and measuring the time it takes for them to bounce back from an object, allowing for precise detection and distance measurement.</p></p>
