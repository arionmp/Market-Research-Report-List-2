<p><p>Leavening acid market types include Anhydrous Monocalcium Phosphate (AMCP), which is a fast-acting, effective leavening agent. Adipic Acid is known for its ability to enhance the texture of baked goods. Fumaric Acid is a popular alternative to traditional leavening agents. Sodium Aluminum Phosphate (SALP) is a versatile leavening acid used in a variety of baking applications. Other types of leavening acids also exist in the market to cater to specific baking needs.</p></p>
