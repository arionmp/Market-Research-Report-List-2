<p><p>Blown Film Coolers (BFC) are specialized machines used in the plastic industry to cool and solidify plastic film as it exits the extrusion process. There are two main types of BFC markets: Built-in Chiller Type and Chilling Pipe Type. The Built-in Chiller Type integrates a chiller unit directly into the cooler system, providing more efficient cooling capabilities. On the other hand, the Chilling Pipe Type uses a series of chilling pipes to cool the plastic film, offering a more cost-effective solution for smaller-scale operations.</p></p>
