<p><strong>The Military Night Vision Binoculars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military night vision binoculars have two main types in the market: image intensifier and thermal image. Image intensifier binoculars work by amplifying ambient light to produce a visible image in low-light conditions. They are effective for short to medium range surveillance. On the other hand, thermal image binoculars detect heat signatures to create an image, allowing for effective surveillance in complete darkness or through barriers like smoke. Both types offer valuable night vision capabilities for military operations.</p></p>
