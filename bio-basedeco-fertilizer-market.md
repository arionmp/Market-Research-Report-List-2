<p><strong>The Bio-based(Eco) Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nitrogen-fixing</li><li>Phosphate-solubilizing</li><li>Potash-mobilizing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-based (Eco) Fertilizer market types include nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, potash-mobilizing, and others. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers contain beneficial bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form that plants can use. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers help plants access phosphorus, an essential nutrient. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers aid in the uptake of potassium, promoting plant growth. Other bio-based fertilizers may include micronutrients, organic matter, or additional beneficial microbes to enhance soil health and plant growth. These different types cater to specific nutrient needs of plants and promote sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
