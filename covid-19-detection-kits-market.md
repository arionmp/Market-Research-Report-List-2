Nucleic Acid Detection Kits identify the genetic material of the virus, providing accurate diagnosis of active infections. Antibody Detection Kits, on the other hand, detect the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus, indicating past or current infection. Both types play a crucial role in managing the COVID-19 pandemic.</p></p>
