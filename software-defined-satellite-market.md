<p><p>Software-Defined Satellite Market Types refer to different orbits where satellites are positioned to provide various services. Medium Earth Orbit satellites are placed at an intermediate altitude, offering a balance between coverage and latency. Low Earth Orbit satellites are positioned closer to the earth, enabling faster communication with lower latency. Geosynchronous Earth Orbit satellites remain stationary above a specific point on the equator, providing continuous coverage to a specific area. Each market type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on coverage, latency, and specific service requirements.</p></p>
