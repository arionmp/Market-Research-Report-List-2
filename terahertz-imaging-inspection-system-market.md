<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz imaging inspection systems can be categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive Terahertz Imaging systems rely on detecting natural Terahertz radiation emitted by objects to create images. In contrast, Active Terahertz Imaging systems emit their own Terahertz radiation and measure the reflected signals to generate images. Both types of systems have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as security, medical imaging, and material inspection. The choice between passive and active systems depends on specific requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
