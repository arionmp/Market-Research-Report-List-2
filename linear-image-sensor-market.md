<p><p>The linear image sensor market is primarily dominated by three types - CMOS, CCD, and others. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption and high integration capabilities, making them ideal for portable devices like smartphones and tablets. CCD sensors, on the other hand, provide better image quality and are often used in professional cameras and scientific equipment. Other types of linear image sensors include hybrid sensors that combine the benefits of both CMOS and CCD technologies, providing unique solutions for specific applications.</p></p>
