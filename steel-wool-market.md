<p><p>Steel wool is categorized into different market types based on the coarseness of the fibers. Super Fine, Extra Fine, and Very Fine steel wool are used for fine polishing and finishing work. Fine and Medium steel wool are commonly used for cleaning and preparing surfaces before painting. Medium Coarse and Coarse steel wool are more abrasive and are used for removing paint or rust. Extra Coarse steel wool is the most aggressive type and is used for heavy-duty cleaning and stripping tasks.</p></p>
