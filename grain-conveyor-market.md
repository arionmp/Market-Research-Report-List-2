<p><p>Grain conveyor market is segmented into three types: Belt type, Roller type, and Others. Belt type conveyors are commonly used for horizontal transport of grains due to their flexibility and high capacity. Roller type conveyors are ideal for inclined or declining transport of grains as they allow for smooth movement. Other types of conveyors may include screw conveyors or bucket elevators which are used for specific applications in the grain industry. Each type of conveyor offers unique advantages depending on the requirements of the operation.</p></p>
