<p><p>Automotive robotics market is categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Articulated robots have multiple joints allowing a high degree of movement and flexibility. Cartesian robots move along three linear axes to perform precise tasks. Cylindrical robots are designed for cylindrical workspaces like painting or welding. SCARA robots have two parallel joints for vertical and horizontal movement. Other types of robots in the market include parallel robots and delta robots, offering unique capabilities for specific automotive tasks.</p></p>
