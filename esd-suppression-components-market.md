There are three main types of ESD suppression components in the market: TVS diodes, metal oxide varistors, and polymeric ESD suppressors. TVS diodes are designed to rapidly respond to ESD events, metal oxide varistors provide protection against high voltage transients, and polymeric ESD suppressors offer long-term reliability and stability. Each type of component caters to different ESD suppression needs in various electronic applications.</p></p>
