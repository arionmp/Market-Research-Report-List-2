<p><p>Excitation loss relays are devices used to protect generators from damage due to loss of excitation. They come in three main types based on the voltage level they operate at: low-voltage type, medium-voltage type, and high-voltage type. Low-voltage type excitation loss relays are used for generators operating at lower voltage levels, typically below 1kV. Medium-voltage type relays are for generators operating at voltages between 1kV and 69kV, while high-voltage type relays are used for generators operating above 69kV.</p></p>
