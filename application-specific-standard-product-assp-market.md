<p><p>Application Specific Standard Product (ASSP) market types include various design methodologies such as standard-cell designs, gate-array and semi-custom design, full-custom design, and structured design. Standard-cell designs involve predefined cells that are interconnected to create custom circuits. Gate-array and semi-custom design allow for limited customization by rearranging pre-designed transistors. Full-custom design offers complete customization for specific applications. Structured design combines standard cells with customizable modules to create complex circuits efficiently. These market types cater to different requirements in terms of cost, performance, and flexibility for various applications.</p></p>
