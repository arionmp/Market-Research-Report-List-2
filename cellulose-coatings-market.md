<p><p>Cellulose coatings refer to coatings made from cellulose, a natural polymer found in plants. There are different types of cellulose coatings, with two common ones being nitrocellulose coatings and cellulose acetate coatings. Nitrocellulose coatings are derived from nitrocellulose, offering fast drying and high gloss finishes. On the other hand, cellulose acetate coatings are made from cellulose acetate, providing durable and glossy finishes. Both types of coatings are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and furniture for their protective and aesthetic properties.</p></p>
