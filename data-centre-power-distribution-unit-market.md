<p><strong>The Data Centre Power Distribution Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-intelligent PDU</li><li>Intelligent PDU</li><li>Metered</li><li>Switched</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Centre Power Distribution Unit market can be categorized into four types: Non-intelligent PDU, Intelligent PDU, Metered, and Switched. Non-intelligent PDUs are basic units that simply distribute power without any monitoring or control functions. Intelligent PDUs provide remote monitoring and control capabilities. Metered PDUs measure power usage at the unit level. Switched PDUs allow for remote power cycling and outlet-level control. Each type offers unique features and benefits to help data centers efficiently manage their power distribution needs.</p></p>
