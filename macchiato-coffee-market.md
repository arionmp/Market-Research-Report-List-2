<p><ul><li>Short Macchiato</li><li>Long Macchiato</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Macchiato coffee comes in two main market types: Short Macchiato and Long Macchiato. Short Macchiato is a single shot of espresso with a small amount of steamed milk, creating a strong and intense flavor profile. Long Macchiato, on the other hand, consists of a double shot of espresso with a larger amount of steamed milk, resulting in a milder and creamier taste. Both market types cater to different preferences, with Short Macchiato appealing to those who enjoy a bold coffee flavor, while Long Macchiato is favored by those who prefer a smoother taste with a hint of sweetness.</p></p>
