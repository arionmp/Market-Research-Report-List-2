<p><p>One component adhesives in the hem flange adhesives market are pre-mixed adhesives that do not require any further mixing before application. They are convenient and easy to use, making them popular for quick and simple applications. On the other hand, two component adhesives in the market require mixing of two separate components before application. While they may be more complex to use, two component adhesives offer stronger and more durable bond compared to one component adhesives, making them suitable for more demanding applications in the hem flange industry.</p></p>
