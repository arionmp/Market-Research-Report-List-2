<p><ul><li>Inorganic Fining Agent</li><li>Organic Fining Agent</li><li>Complex Fining Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wine clarifiers market includes three main types: inorganic fining agents, organic fining agents, and complex fining agents. Inorganic fining agents, such as bentonite, silica gel, and activated carbon, are commonly used to remove unwanted compounds from wine. Organic fining agents, like gelatin and egg whites, help to clarify wine by binding to particulates. Complex fining agents are a combination of both inorganic and organic materials. Each type of wine clarifier offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the winemaker.</p></p>
