<p><ul><li>Fast Blow</li><li>Medium/Normal Blow</li><li>Time Delay/Slow Blow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cartridge fuses are available in three main types based on their blowing characteristics: fast blow, medium/normal blow, and time delay/slow blow. Fast blow fuses are designed to quickly interrupt the circuit in case of overcurrent. Medium/normal blow fuses provide a moderate response time to protect against short circuits. Time delay/slow blow fuses have a delayed reaction time, ideal for protecting against temporary overloads. Each type is designed to cater to different voltage and current requirements, providing versatile protection for various electronic applications.</p></p>
