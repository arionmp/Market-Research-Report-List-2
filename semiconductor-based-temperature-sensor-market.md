<p><strong>The Semiconductor based Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Current Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Digital Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors</li><li>Diode Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor-based temperature sensors are categorized based on their output signal types. Voltage output temperature sensors provide a voltage signal proportional to temperature, while current output temperature sensors give a current output. Digital output temperature sensors offer temperature readings in digital format. Resistance output silicon temperature sensors change resistance with temperature. Diode temperature sensors utilize the temperature-dependent voltage across a diode junction. Each type serves specific applications and industries, offering a range of features and benefits to meet various temperature sensing needs.</p></p>
