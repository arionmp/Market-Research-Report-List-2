<p><strong>The Smart Window Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Adhesive Film</li><li>None-Adhesive Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart window film is categorized into two types based on the adhesive used: self-adhesive film and non-adhesive film. Self-adhesive film is coated with a sticky backing, allowing for easy application and removal without the need for additional adhesives. On the other hand, non-adhesive film utilizes static cling technology to adhere to windows, making it easy to reposition and reuse. Both types of smart window film offer benefits such as privacy, UV protection, and energy efficiency, with the choice between adhesive types depending on the desired installation method and convenience.</p></p>
