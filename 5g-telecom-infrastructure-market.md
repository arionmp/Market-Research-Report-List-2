<p><p>Femtocell, Pico Cell, and Micro Cell are types of 5G telecom infrastructure markets that cater to different coverage areas. Femtocells are small, low-power base stations designed for home or small office use. Pico cells cover slightly larger areas such as shopping malls or airports. Micro cells provide coverage over even larger areas like urban regions or college campuses. Each market type serves a specific purpose in providing seamless connectivity and better network performance for users in different environments.</p></p>
