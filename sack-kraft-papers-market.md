<p><p>Sack Kraft Papers can be classified into two main types: Bleached Kraft Paper and Natural Kraft Paper. Bleached Kraft Paper undergoes a bleaching process to achieve a white or light-colored appearance, making it suitable for packaging products where a clean and polished look is desired. On the other hand, Natural Kraft Paper maintains its original brown color, giving it a more rustic and eco-friendly appeal. Both types of papers are commonly used in the packaging industry for applications such as grocery bags, cement sacks, and animal feed packaging.</p></p>
