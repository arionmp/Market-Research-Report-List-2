<p><p>Plastic mould market consists of various types including Compression Moulding Moulds, Extrusion Moulding Moulds, Injection Moulding Moulds, Blow Moulding Moulds, and Foam Moulding Moulds. Compression moulding involves compressing plastic material into a mold cavity. Extrusion moulding creates hollow shapes by forcing molten plastic through a die. Injection moulding injects molten plastic into a mold cavity. Blow moulding forms hollow objects by inflating a heated plastic tube. Foam moulding creates foam products by injecting gas into a molten plastic.</p></p>
