<p><p>The wireless speaker market is divided into three main types based on the technology they use: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Infrared. Bluetooth technology allows speakers to connect wirelessly to devices within a short range, typically up to 30 feet. Wi-Fi technology enables speakers to connect to a network and stream music from various devices. Infrared technology uses signals to transmit audio between devices in line of sight. Each technology offers different features and benefits, catering to various consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
