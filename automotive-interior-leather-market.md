<p><p>Automotive interior leather market consists of two main types: Genuine leather and Synthetic leather. Genuine leather is made from animal hides and is known for its durability, luxury appearance, and breathable nature. It is often used in high-end vehicles due to its premium quality. On the other hand, Synthetic leather, also known as faux leather, is made from synthetic materials like polyurethane. It is more affordable and easier to maintain compared to genuine leather. Both types cater to different market segments based on their price, quality, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
