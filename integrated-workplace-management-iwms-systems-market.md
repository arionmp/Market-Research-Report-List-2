Cloud-based IWMS systems are hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility and scalability. On-Premises IWMS systems are installed and maintained on the organization's own servers, offering more control over data security and customization. Both market types cater to different organizational needs and preferences in managing workplace resources effectively.</p></p>
