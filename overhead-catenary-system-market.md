<p><p>The Overhead Catenary System market can be categorized into two types: Flexible Overhead Catenary System and Rigid Overhead Catenary System. Flexible Overhead Catenary Systems use flexible supports such as wires or cables to provide power to trains, allowing for more adaptability in the system's design. On the other hand, Rigid Overhead Catenary Systems use rigid supports like poles or beams to deliver power, offering stability and consistency in power distribution. Both systems play a crucial role in powering electric trains efficiently.</p></p>
