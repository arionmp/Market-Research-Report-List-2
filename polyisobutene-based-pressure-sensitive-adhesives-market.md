<p><p>Polyisobutene-based pressure-sensitive adhesives are commonly found in both waterborne and solvent borne forms in the market. Waterborne adhesives utilize water as the dispersing medium, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. On the other hand, solvent borne adhesives use organic solvents for dispersion, making them suitable for applications where fast drying is required. Both types of adhesives offer excellent tackiness and adhesion properties, making them popular choices in various industries such as packaging, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
