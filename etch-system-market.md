ICP systems use plasma to etch materials, RIE systems use reactive gases to remove material, and DRIE systems provide deep and precise etching capabilities. Other types of etch systems may include plasma-based, ion beam, or wet etching technologies. Each type of system offers unique advantages and is used in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, MEMS fabrication, and microelectronics.</p></p>
